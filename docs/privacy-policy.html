Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Prayer Quest is a family app. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work, we never sell your data, and children's devices require no account of any kind.

1. Who We Are

Prayer Quest is developed and operated by Kareem Fares ("we", "us", or "our"). You can reach us at kareem.mfares@gmail.com.

2. What We Collect

Parent accounts — when a parent creates an account we collect:

  • Email address and display name (via email/password sign-up, Sign in with Apple, or Google Sign-In)
  • Children's first names, ages, and gender (entered by the parent)
  • Prayer completion records for each child
  • Reward goals set by the parent

Child devices — children join via a 6-character invite code shared by the parent. No email, password, or personal information is required from the child. The child's device stores only a local session identifier (the child's ID) to restore their session.

We do not collect:

  • Location data
  • Device contacts or photos
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any data from children under 13 beyond what the parent explicitly enters

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To display prayer progress on parent and child devices
  • To sync data between a parent's account and linked child devices
  • To restore sessions when the app is reopened

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the app.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication & Firestore database) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Sign-In — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Sign in with Apple — apple.com/legal/privacy

Data stored in Firebase is held in Google's secure cloud infrastructure. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

5. Camera Permission

The app requests camera access solely to scan the QR code displayed by the parent's device. This is an optional convenience — the child can also type the 6-character invite code manually. No photos are taken or stored.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Prayer Quest is designed for use by parents on behalf of their children. Parents control all data entered for their children. Child devices operate without any account and cannot submit personal information independently. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child without parental consent, please contact us at kareem.mfares@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account exists. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at kareem.mfares@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

8. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Google Firebase with access restricted by server-side security rules. Parent data is accessible only to the authenticated parent. Child prayer logs are accessible only to the parent who created the child profile.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at kareem.mfares@gmail.com.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
